What we stand for and who we are

We at Beyond Data believe that knowledge is power and that converting data into eaily readable formats aids understanding. We are here to help you understand the universe beyond our atmosphere in the hopes that it will make humanities transition to life beyond earth just a little bit easier.

Beyond Data is a data visualisation and art corp. that uses NASA API to create visualisations and data centered art to help our fellow humans educate themselves on all things space related. We want to spark an excitement about space exploration and make more knowledge more easily accessible.
